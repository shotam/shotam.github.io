Shota Momma 門馬将太

I am a post-doctoral researcher at University of California, San Diego. I study sentence comprehension, sentence production, and the relationship between the two. I am specifically interested in the cognitive mechanism for assembling sentence structures in speaking and understanding, and whether we need two mechanisms (one for speaking and the other for understanding) or just one to do so.
